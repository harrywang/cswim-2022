Registration

Because of the current pandemic control policy, the conference size is currently limited to 150 offline participants. Therefore, priority will be given to the first 150 participants who registered for in-person participation (no limit for online participation), but we will keep other participants (who registered for in-person participation later than 150th) in the waiting list. A confirmation message will be sent to the first 150 in-person participants. We will closely monitor the situation and update you if there is any change in the pandemic control policy (refund will be made to participants who registered later than 150th by end July if the policy remains unchanged by then). We sincerely thank you for your kind understanding.
